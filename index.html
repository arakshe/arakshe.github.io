Group Members: Anvi Rakshe, Manika Sakulsureeyadej, Mehek Daga

Project Description: This Sankey diagram visualizes how resources flow across categories and helps identify key transitions and bottlenecks.

About the Visualization

The Sankey diagram above represents the flow of resources from their origin points to their destinations. The width of each link is proportional to the quantity of the flow, allowing viewers to quickly identify major contributors, losses, or inefficiencies in the system.

Our visualization helps analyze complex processes by simplifying them into a readable flow chart. For example, this can be applied to energy systems, budget allocation, user journeys, or supply chains to spot trends and make data-driven decisions.

This project was created using D3.js and focuses on clarity, accessibility, and interactivity. Hovering over elements (if supported) reveals details about specific transitions.
