Startup Funding Visualizations

Team: Anvi Rakshe, Manika Sakulsureeyadej, Mehek Daga

About: Interactive visualizations of startup funding rounds and sources.

Sankey Diagram Insights

Visualizes funding stages (1–3, 4–6, 7+ rounds) and sources, highlighting venture capital dominance and funding diversification at later stages.

Funding Round Distribution

Shows startup distribution by funding rounds, indicating majority early-stage funding and challenges in later stages.

Funding Amount Variability

Displays funding amount distributions, highlighting medians, quartiles, and significant outliers.

Geographic Distribution (SF)

Interactive map showcasing startup concentration and funding patterns in the SF Bay Area.

Startup Age vs Funding

Explores correlation between startup age, funding, and status (acquired, operating, closed).
